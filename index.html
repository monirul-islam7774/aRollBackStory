Story

This is a story of two nation. Just like every other story one nation attacked the other one. The attacked nation is in turmoils. Our hero appears out of nowhere to save the day. But little did he know the game developer was not on his side.Can our hero overcome the hardships and save his nation? Find that out by guiding our hero on his epic journey.

Gameplay

Guide the ball left or right and jump to make progress. You will have to interact with the world to move forward. And Try Not to die too much , Else the game might break......
